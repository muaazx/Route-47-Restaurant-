OUR HIGHWAY HISTORY

EVERY BITE IS
WORTH THE JOURNEY

Route47 is Pakistan's fastest-growing gourmet burger destination, engineered specifically for food enthusiasts who refuse to compromise on flavor, quality, and crunch.

Founded with a passion for roadside diners, street foods, and night highway road-tripping, our mission was simple: bypass the corporate fast-food freezer culture. We craft 100% prime certified halal beef ground fresh daily, smash it onto blisteringly hot cast-iron griddles to lock in caramelization, and cradle them inside pillow-soft baked brioche buns.

We are more than just a menu; we are an entire road-trip mood. From Lahore to Karachi and Islamabad, we represent the culinary freedom of the open road.
